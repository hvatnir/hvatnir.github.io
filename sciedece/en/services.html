Laser implantation

Laser implantation promotes rapid healing of the wound surface. The sterility of the operating field is provided not only medication, but also the action of laser to the cutting zone. Reduced risk of bleeding is minimized, recovery processes with a smaller by inflammation and in a shorter period. Thus, by non-traumatic, painless and safe procedure, our experienced surgeons to easily install the implant, which will serve as a long and trouble-free without complication.

Ultrasonic implantation

When you need ultra-precise and safe positioning of the implant, the bone splitting and increasing in of bone its volume (without soft tissue injuries), ultrasound implantation is the best option. This type of intervention is exclusively two-step. Prosthetics is possible only within 2-4 months after surgery. The procedure is the most non-invasive, minor postoperative swelling, and implant survival rate is much higher. We provide modern and reliable method of implantation in difficult anatomical conditions.

Bone grafting

It is safe, painless and safely conducted operation of direct regeneration of bone tissue in maxillofacial area, replantation of bone block, splitting or displacement of the alveolar ridge for creating the conditions subsequent implantation. We work with materials of European production, industry leaders of Italy, Germany and Switzerland. The medical center also provides autograft bone implantation, sinus lifting.

A-PRF, I-PRF

Plazmolifting is an innovative therapeutic and rejuvenating injection procedure involving subcutaneous injection of platelet-rich plasma of the patient's own blood. A-PRF - usage of the patient's blood plasma in the form of membranes. I-Prf - patient plasma product, which is used to stabilize the bone regeneration material. Application of the PRF cell is stimulated growth, resulting in improval of blood flow, strengthening of the bone, decrease of the inflammatory process.

Computed Tomography (CBCT) 3D

Computed Tomography (CBCT) of the two jaws and joints

This is a method of X-ray examination for the purpose of diagnosis, monitoring and treatment planning. The scanning process takes 30 seconds, after which we get a 3D image with a volume of 8 cm x 23 cm in three-dimensional projection, which allows us to explore the maxillofacial area invisible to the human eye, maxillary sinuses and joints with the maximum information content.

A safe examination is performed on a modern high-precision tomograph with a minimum load and does not have consequences on your body.

We recommend that, in order to monitor the condition of the teeth, as well as to monitor the stability of implants in the bones, to conduct this type of examination once a year as a prevention of complications and the development of chronic foci of jaw infection.
